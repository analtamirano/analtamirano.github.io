I love watching movies in my spare time and one of my favorite genre of movies is sci-fi/fantasy. The reason I love sci-fi and fantasy so much is because I love the often futuristic and apocolyptic feel these movies have. When I watch movies, I want to forget about real world problems for a while and be transported into an entirely different place. One of my favorite sci-fi fantasy movies is Blade Runner 2049. It is a sequel to the 1980's classic Blade Runner and continues the story of the complex relationship between replicants and humans. With director Denis Villeneuve, cinematographor Roger Deakins, and a score by Hans Zimmer, this movie gets as close to perfect as possible.
